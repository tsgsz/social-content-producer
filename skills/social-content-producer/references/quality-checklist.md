### Compliance Checks

#### Forbidden Words
- [ ] No "最" series (最好, 最大, 最适合, etc.)
- [ ] No "第一" series
- [ ] No absolute promises (保证, 肯定, 一定)
- [ ] No exaggerated claims (闭眼跟, 必看)
- [ ] No extreme adjectives (极致, 颠覆, 完美)

#### AI-Toned Language
- [ ] No "综上所述/本文将/以下是"
- [ ] No mechanical parallel structures
- [ ] No template openings
- [ ] First-person experience included
- [ ] Conversational tone verified

#### Platform Compliance
- [ ] WeChat: Practical, tool-focused
- [ ] Xiaohongshu: Screenshot-worthy, info-dense
- [ ] Douyin: Fast-paced, hook-driven

### Specific Details Check
